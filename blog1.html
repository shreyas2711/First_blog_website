Nokia X10, X20 and XR20 are receiving new Android 12 Build updates while Nokia 7.2 is getting July security patch. Nokia XR20 just received it here in Croatia, but there are many reports about its availability so check your phone for updates. The new build 2.410 is is 85.25 MB large and it brings the usual UI enhancements and system stability together with the June security patch. I’d love to see what exactly was fixed or at least something more than the usual seen on the screen.

On the other hand, the Build updates for Nokia X10 and X20 are somewhat larger. The update for X10 is 174 MB in size while the update for X20 is 275 MB large. The new build is bringing June security patches with some UI enhancements and system stability.
